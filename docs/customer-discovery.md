## Priority verticals (current)

Based on discovery conversations, the verticals with the clearest path to a pilot:

1. **CNC / precision machining** — lowest friction, operators already track spindle hours
2. **Food & beverage** — strong second; regulatory pressure on equipment uptime creates budget
3. **Pharmaceutical** — high compliance requirements make documented RUL forecasts valuable
4. **HVAC** — large install base, maintenance-heavy
5. **Maritime / shipping** — longer sales cycles but strong signal from fleet maintenance leads
