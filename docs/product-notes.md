# Product notes

## What Kaful is

Kaful (kaful.ai) is industrial AI software that automatically generates prognostic digital twins for rotating industrial equipment from OEM documentation, forecasting remaining useful life as a probability range without requiring historical failure data.

## Who it's for

Maintenance engineers and reliability teams at small and mid-sized manufacturers who:
- Operate equipment without a historical failure dataset
- Are installing new machines with no track record
- Want component-level failure forecasts they can act on, not black-box predictions
- Can't justify a 2-year data collection project before getting value from predictive maintenance

## How it works (one paragraph)

Kaful ingests the OEM manual for a piece of equipment, extracts component specs and failure modes using retrieval-augmented generation, constructs a physics-based degradation simulation, and runs a particle filter to produce a calibrated RUL probability range per component. The entire process is automated — no manual configuration per machine type.
